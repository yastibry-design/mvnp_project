About the Repository

Supporting science-based conservation and protected area management.

Overview

The Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park (MVNP) Research Repository serves as the Official Archive of Research Studies, Technical Reports, Biodiversity Assessments, Academic Thesis, Journal Articles, and other scientific outputs conducted within Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park.

The repository provides centralized access to knowledge products that support Biodiversity Conservation, Ecosystem Management, Environmental Policy Development, Research Collaboration, and evidence-based decision-making within the protected area.

Objectives

  • Preserve scientific knowledge generated within MVNP.
  • Support research transparency and accessibility.
  • Facilitate information sharing among researchers and stakeholders.
  • Strengthen evidence-based conservation planning.
  • Promote scientific collaboration and innovation.

Research Governance

All research conducted within Mahagnao Volcano Natural Park shall comply with applicable Philippine Laws and DENR Policies, including the Expanded National Integrated Protected Areas System Act (R.A 11038), the Wildlife Resources Conservation and Protection Act (R.A 9147), and relevant DENR administrative issuances.

Research proposals are reviewed under the MVNP Research Guidelines and corresponding procedures established by the Protected Area Management Board (PAMB) and Protected Area Management Office (PAMO).
